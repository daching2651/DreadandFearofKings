About

Designed for men whose job it is to provide and maintain security for their clients in a discreet posture, “The Dread and Fear of Kings” is a brand that was intended to cater to a few small, niche groups of individuals. The first group is those who are actively involved in careers that require subtle but functional clothing. Some examples of this group are individuals who are currently working in the military and law enforcement in a covert capacity. Another is individuals who work in the private security sector who need to be able to have functionality and capability built into their clothing to avoid drawing attention by wearing a standardized uniform. Lastly, the brand is focused on individuals who want to invest in quality durable clothing that has been tested to withstand abuse while offering increased capability to the wearer. Where other designer clothes and accessories for men tends to emphasize aesthetics above all, we ensure that form follows function..
